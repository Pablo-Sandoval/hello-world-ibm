How It Works

From signal to actionable intelligence.

Our end-to-end pipeline turns raw radar returns into operationally ready intelligence in milliseconds.

01
📡
Signal Emission

Our phased-array antennas emit precisely shaped RF pulses, optimised for the target environment and operational band.

02
📶
Return Processing

Reflected signals are captured and fed into our FPGA-accelerated signal processor, filtering clutter and noise in real time.

03
🧠
AI Classification

Our onboard AI classifies detected objects by type, trajectory, and threat level using a library of 50,000+ radar signatures.

04
📊
Intelligence Delivery

Processed tracks and alerts are pushed to command systems, dashboards, or third-party C2 platforms via secure APIs.
